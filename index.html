Tonga

Tonga is a Polynesian kingdom in the South Pacific. Tonga is known for its rich cultural heritage, including traditional music and dance. The economy is primarily based on agriculture, tourism, and remittances from Tongans living abroad. The Tongan flag reflects the Christian faith and identity of the Tongan people.
